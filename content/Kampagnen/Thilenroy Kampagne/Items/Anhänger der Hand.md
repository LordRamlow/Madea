Dieser Anhänger war an einer silbernen Kette um [[DM Stuff/SCs/Thilenroy Kampagne II/Renault de Vaunt|Renault de Vaunts]] Hals, als [[content/Kampagnen/Thilenroy Kampagne/SCs/Oliver De Vaunt]] ihn gefunden hatte. Es ist aus einem dunklen, scheinbar uralten Metall und hat die Form einer Skeletthand. 

_As a reaction, if you or any creature you see within 30 feet of you takes necrotic damage, you can half that damage. Also, the first time you hit with a spell attack on your next turn, the target takes an extra 1d6 necrotic damage. This item has five charges and using the effect expends one of them. They all recharge each dawn._
