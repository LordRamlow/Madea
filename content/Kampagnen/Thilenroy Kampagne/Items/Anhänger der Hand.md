Dieser Anhänger war an einer silbernen Kette um [[Renault de Vaunt|Renault de Vaunts]] Hals, als [[Oliver De Vaunt]] ihn gefunden hatte. Es ist aus einem dunklen, scheinbar uralten Metall und hat die Form einer Skeletthand. 

_As a reaction, if you or any creature you see within 30 feet of you takes necrotic damage, you can half that damage. Also, the first time you hit with a spell attack or melee attack on your next turn, the target takes an extra 1d8 necrotic damage + your charisma modifier. This item has three charges and using the effect expends one of them. They all recharge each dawn. You can also expend a charge to add 1d6 + your Charisma Modifier necrotic damage to any damage roll you make._
