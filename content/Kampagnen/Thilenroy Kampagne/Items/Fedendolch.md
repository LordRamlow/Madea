**_Requires Attunement_**_: Yes_ 

Dieser Dolch wurde von einem zwergischen Meisterschmied geschmiedet, der mit magischen Runen den Namen einer Person (in diesem Fall [[Don’al Athon von den steinigen Ebenen]]) eingraviert hat.

_The wielder attunes to the Grudge Knife by swearing vengeance upon the wronged and their kin. Once attuned, you gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. 

On a hit, the target takes an additional 1d6 psychic damage from the force of the knife’s rage. 

**_Special_**_. Blood Feud: against the person whom the grudge was sworn against, or anyone who shares their blood, the knife becomes a +2 magic weapon and the extra psychic damage increases to 3d6. If the target of the grudge and their whole family line dies, the knife reverts to being a non-magical blade.__
