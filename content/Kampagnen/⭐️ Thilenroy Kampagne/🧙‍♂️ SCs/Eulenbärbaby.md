**Armor Class** 13 (natural armor)  
**Hit Points** 19 (3d10 + 3)  
**Speed** 30 ft.

SPECIAL TRAITS
- **Keen Sight and Smell**. The owlbear has advantage on [Wisdom](https://www.5esrd.com/using-ability-scores#TOC-Wisdom) ([Perception](https://www.5esrd.com/using-ability-scores#Perception)) checks that rely on sight or smell.
- **Mighty Build**. The owlbear counts as one size larger when determining its carrying capacity and the weight it can push, drag, or lift.

ACTIONS
- **Beak**. _Melee Weapon Attack_: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. _Hit_: 6 (1d6 + 3) piercing damage.
- **Claws**. _Melee Weapon Attack_: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit_: 5 (1d4 + 3) slashing damage.
