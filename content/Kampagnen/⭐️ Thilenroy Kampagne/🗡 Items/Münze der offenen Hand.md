Dieses Amulett wurde immer von Master Mohli getragen, es stellt eine offene Hand dar. Es war immer sehr wichtig für ihn und daher wollte [[Don’al Athon von den steinigen Ebenen|Don]] es nicht zurücklassen. 

_When you take damage by a melee attack, you can reduce the damage to zero. Add 1d6 of that same damage type you took to every damage roll you make until the end of the next turn and subtract the amount from the damage you took with the hit. At the end of your next turn, you then take the remaining damage. This effect can be used thrice and then recharges at the next dawn._
