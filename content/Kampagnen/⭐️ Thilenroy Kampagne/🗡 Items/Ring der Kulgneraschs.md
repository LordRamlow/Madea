Dieser Ring ist ein altes Familienerbstück, das [[Norgrim Kulgnerasch]] von seinem Vater erhielt. Dieser prunkvolle Bartring bildet das Wappen der Familie Kulgnerasch ab. 

_As a reaction to any attack, you can call forth the spirit of all your ancestors and manifest it through a mighty scream. Every creature who hears that scream and which you choose gains resistance to nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing or slashing damage (you choose) till the start of your next turn. This effect can't be used again until the next dawn._
