Dieses Schild ist ein fachmännisch gefertigtes Schild, das zusätzlich noch mit einem Zauber belegt wurde. Es ist besonders gut geeignet gegen Fernkampfwaffen. 


Armor (shield), rare (requires attunement)

_You gain a +2 bonus to [Armor Class](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#ArmorClass) against ranged attack rolls while you wield this Shield. This bonus is in addition to the Shield’s normal bonus to AC. Whenever an attacker makes a ranged attack roll against a target within 5 feet of you, you can take a [Reaction](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Reaction) to become the target of the attack instead. Shields require the [Utilize](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#UtilizeAction) action to Don or Doff. You gain the Armor Class benefit of a Shield only if you have training with it.
